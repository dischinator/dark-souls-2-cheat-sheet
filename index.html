Cemetery of Ash

  • From the start head right at the first turn and pick up the Soul of a Deserted Corpse
  • Loot the corpse on the fountain to obtain the Ashen Estus Flask
  • Turn right after the fountain and head down the path through a flooded passage. Kill the Ravenous Crystal Lizard to get a Titanite Scale and then pick up the Soul of an Unknown Traveler from the corpse in the area
  • Head back to the fountain and take the path straight ahead. Climb the cliff and rest at the Cemetery of Ash bonfire to receive the Rest gesture
  • From the bonfire head down the left path and jump from the cliff over to the corpse on the tomb to pick up a Titanite Shard
  • Take the side ridge down from the large cliff face on the right to pick up 5 Firebombs from a nearby corpse
  • Back on the main path head through the archway and take the sword embedded in the statue. Kill Iudex Gundyr to receive the Coiled Sword
  • Congratulate yourself on beating the first boss of Dark Souls 3 and rest at the Iudex Gundyr bonfire
  • Continue through the large door and pick up the Broken Straight Sword from the corpse leaning on the tombstone
  • Head over to the large cliff edge and pick up the Homeward Bone at the edge of the ridge
  • Take the left path up the cliff and turn left just before Firelink Shrine and grab the East-West Shield from the corpse on the tree
  • Kill Sword Master Saber to get the Uchigatana, Master's Attire, and Master's Gloves, and to unlock his summon sign
  • Head back and up the path to the left to pick up an Ember from a corpse above the entrance above Firelink Shrine
  • Continue across to the other side picking up a Homeward Bone from a corpse behind a tomb and another Ember from the lower ridge
  • Go back to the main path and enter Firelink Shrine

Firelink Shrine

  • Talk to Fire Keeper
  • Talk to Shrine Handmaid
  • Talk to Hawkwood to receive Collapse Gesture
  • Talk to Blacksmith Andre to receive Hurrah! Gesture
  • Go out the top of Firelink shrine and pick up the Soul of a Deserted Corpse from the corpse next to the small decrepit tower
  • When you have the Tower Key, unlock the small decrepit tower and head up to the bridge leading to the Bell Tower
  • Roll off the bridge onto the Firelink Shrine roof and kick down the ladder shortcut
  • Just behind the roof kill the Crystal Lizard to get a Twinkling Titanite
  • Pick up the Homeward Bone from the lower part of the roof
  • Inside the roof pick up the Estus Flask Shard from up in the rafters
  • Trade any possible item with Pickle-Pee, Pump-a-Rum crow to receive the Call Over gesture
  • Hit the Illusory Wall at the back of the rafters and drop down to get the Covetous Silver Serpent Ring from the chest
  • Head into the Bell Tower and look down to the left. Carefully jump down to the tombstone and pick up the Fire Keeper Set from the corpse
  • Make your way down to the bottom and pick up the Estus Ring from another corpse
  • Open the door and head back to the Bell Tower. Go up the lift and Patches may close the door depending on your progress in the game
  • At the top of the Bell Tower, pick up the Fire Keeper Soul

High Wall of Lothric

  • Open the door ahead and rest at the High Wall of Lothric bonfire
  • Take the right path onto the rampart and head down the small stairs on the left. Pick up the Soul of a Deserted Corpse from the corpse
  • Head back up the stairs and up the next set of stairs at the end of the rampart. If you want a Titanite Shard and an Ember, wait for one of the undead to transform into Pus of Man and then kill it. Also, pick up the Longbow and 12 Standard Arrows from the corpse
  • Go back to the bonfire and this time head down the left path. Pick up the Soul of a Deserted Corpse from the corpse
  • Head up the stairs at the end of the rampart and up some more stairs to the top of the turret. Pick up the Binoculars from the corpse
  • Go back downstairs and drop down behind the dragon. Enter the small doorway to pick up two Gold Pine Resins from the corpse
  • Drop into the indoor room and pick up two Firebombs from the corpse beside the table
  • Go down the ladder and pick up the Soul of a Deserted Corpse from the corpse outside of the cell
  • Snipe the dragon until it drops the Large Titanite Shard and flies away
  • Grab the Claymore, Club, Ember and Large Soul of a Deserted Corpse from in front of where the dragon was
  • Open the door underneath where the dragon was. Go downstairs. Either kill the Mimic or hit it with a Undead Hunter Charm to get the Deep Battle Axe
  • Head out the door on the other side and pick up the Soul of a Deserted Corpse from the corpse on the left
  • Go into the next building and pick up the three Firebombs from the wooden beam
  • Go out the side exit and up the stairs and pick up the Titanite Shard from the corpse. Light the Tower on the Wall bonfire
  • After lighting the Tower on the Wall bonfire, return to Firelink Shrine and talk to Ringfinger Leonhard who gives you five Cracked Red-Eye Orbs. He'll be by Prince Lothric's throne, if he isn't at this point, try killing Vordt and check again
  • Back inside the building go down the stairs and down the ladder. Go into the next room and pick up eight Throwing Knives on the corpse
  • Continue down the passageway and pick up the Mail Breaker opposite the door on a corpse. Continue down this area to find a cell
  • Free Greirat of the Undead Settlement's from the cell with Cell Key, which is found at the bottom of the building that is past the Tower on the Wall bonfire. Talk to him. If you agree to find Loretta, he gives you the Blue Tearstone Ring. He then moves to Firelink Shrine
  • Go back to the ladder and climb up. Go out the side door and pick up the Soul of a Deserted Corpse from the corpse
  • Head onto the roof. If you want a Titanite Shard and an Ember, wait for one of the undead to transform into Pus of Man and then kill it. And pick up three Firebombs from the corpse. Continue on and kill the Crystal Lizard to receive a Raw Gem. There is another corpse on the roof which holds a Large Soul of a Deserted Corpse
  • Take the ladder down and go onto the side roof to get three Black Firebombs from the corpse
  • On the adjacent balcony with the archers pick up three Firebombs from the corpse
  • Go back inside the earlier building next to the ladder. Take the immediate left and roll into the pots to get two Undead Hunter Charms from the corpse. Continue inside and grab the Titanite Shard from the corpse while being cautious of the ambush
  • Back in the main room pick up the Soul of a Deserted Corpse from the corpse tucked inside the first corner
  • Continue through the doorway and take the next door on the left. Pick up the Broadsword from the corpse
  • Head down the stairs taking the right path and get a Silver Eagle Kite Shield from the chest
  • Head back to the original entrance find the doorway the right blocked by debris. Roll through it and pick up two Green Blossom at the end of the ledge
  • Drop down and pick up the Astora Straight Sword from the chest
  • Continue into the room and pick up the Cell Key and Titanite Shard from the corpses around the room
  • Grab Estus Flask Shard on an altar in the room where you get the Cell Key
  • Head out the lower level and drop down to pick up the Rapier next to the drain
  • Collect the two Embers on corpses around the fountain
  • Head out towards the courtyard and turn left to pick up the Soul of a Deserted Corpse from a corpse
  • Go upstairs right of the courtyard entrance and head straight along the path. After leaving the first door immediately turn left to find three Green Blossom on a corpse
  • Activate the elevator to open up a shortcut from the High Wall bonfire. Behind the elevator at the top are six Throwing Knives on a corpse
  • Head back towards the main courtyard but this time take the stairs to the left overlooking the fountain area. Grab the Large Soul of a Deserted Corpse on the rooftop
  • Jump onto the awning to pick up a Ring of Sacrifice
  • Continue back to the courtyard. In the corner of the terrace just before the stairs leading down, you can summon Lion Knight Albert
  • Head to the left. Go up the stairs on the left just before the cathedral and kill the Red-Eyed Royal Guard to get a Refined Gem
  • Inside the chapel talk to Emma, High Priestess of Lothric Castle to receive the Way of Blue covenant and the Small Lothric Banner
  • Heading out from the chapel take the left stairs and pick up the Lucerne from the corpse
  • Just before walking through great gate, you can summon Sword Master Saber if he was killed earlier at Firelink Shrine
  • Proceed through the gate and kill Vordt of the Boreal Valley. After killing Vordt, raise the banner at the cliff to access the Undead Settlement

Undead Settlement

  • After the Bat Wing Demons have dropped you off, walk to the left, and grab a Large Souls of a Deserted Corpse
  • Go downstairs. Walk behind an overturned carriage and kill 2 Dogs to get 2 Alluring Skulls
  • Find Yoel of Londor below the Foot of the High Wall bonfire amongst several other Pilgrims. Talk to him and agree to his request and he moves to Firelink Shrine. Head back to Firelink Shrine and talk to Yoel to receive the Beckon Gesture. Accept Yoel's offer to Draw Out True Strength which gains you a free level and a Dark Sigil. From now on, for each time you die, you gain 1 Hollowing. Yoel offers 4 more free levels at 2, 6, 12, and 16 Hollowing, so getting all 5 Dark Sigils should not take long. If you do not get all 5 Dark Sigils before reaching the Catacombs of Carthus, Yoel dies which voids the Usurpation of Fire Ending
  • After you get all 5 Dark Sigils and refresh the world, Yoel dies with Yuria of Londor watching over his newfound corpse. Talk to her to receive the Dignified Bow Gesture and buy the Londor Braille Divine Tome
  • From the Undead Settlement bonfire, enter the house. Cut down a glowing hanging corpse to get the Small Leather Shield
  • Walk downstairs to get 2 Charcoal Pine Bundles. Walk out onto the balcony. Turn right around the corner to get 2 Repair Powder
  • Cut down the hanging body and then grab Loretta's bone. Return to Firelink Shrine and give the bone to Greirat. He then allows you to keep the Blue Tearstone Ring
  • Walk downstairs to get 2 more Charcoal Pine Bundles
  • Grab Estus Shard by the burning tree with worshipping undead
  • On the other side of the tree, grab an Ember. Shoot down a hanging corpse from the tree to get 9 Kukris. Walk to the close gate to grab a Soul of an Unknown Traveler
  • Walk to the right of the house to grab a Titanite Shard
  • From the burning tree, walk across the bridge and walk around the right which should be blocked by wooden crates and barrels. Walk towards the houses with the Thralls. Walk around one of the houses to get the Plank Shield. Walk onto the rooftop of the house to your left. Grab 6 Firebombs
  • Climb onto the rooftop of the other house. Grab 2 Homeward Bones. Jump onto the ledge where a Cage Carrier is walking around. Cut down the hanging corpse and grab the Flame Stoneplate Ring. If you are unable to grab the ring and the corpse falls down, you can get the ring by proceeding towards the tower, Eygon, and Irina without resting at a bonfire
  • Covenant: Climb onto the cage of the Cage-Carrier. After you get out of your cage, walk towards the light. Grab the Wargod Wooden Shield. Talk to Holy Knight Hodrick to receive the Mound-Maker covenant and a Homeward Bone. If you have reached the Curse-Rotted Greatwood before reaching the Cage Carrier, Hodrick dies, and the Cage Carrier becomes hostile. Thus, you cannot acquire the covenant until later through Sirris' sidequest
  • Walk back to the house across the bridge. Instead of walking around the house, this time, walk into the house to get a Large Soul of a Deserted Corpse. Then open the backdoor
  • From your right, get the Caduceus Round Shield
  • Walk across the bridge towards the house in view and jump down to the ledge on your right. When you are on the ledge, walk towards the bridge to get a Titanite Shard
  • Walk to the top of the house and find a cage that imprisons Cornyx of the Great Swamp. Talk to him, and he moves to Firelink Shrine
  • Grab the Hand Axe and get a Partizan from a nearby hanging body
  • Walk down from the house and walk onto the gallow. Grab a Soul of an Unknown Traveler. Walk across the unfinished bridge to get the Fire Clutch Ring
  • Walk through the archway of the house and down the stairs. Walk left and around the corner to get a Large Soul of a Deserted Corpse. Walk downstairs and towards a tree with a Dog. Kill it for an Ember
  • Run towards bridge, and enter the sewers on the right. Kill a Giant Rat to get a Bloodbite Ring. Grab Caestus and climb up to open the shortcut
  • Walk back through the sewers and onto the bridge to get another Ember. Walk upstairs towards the house on your right. Grab a Large Soul of a Deserted Corpse. Walk around the rightside of the house to get 3 Alluring Skulls
  • Walk towards the tower. Talk to Eygon of Carim outside and Irina of Carim in her cell. If you have enough health, you can drop into the valley to reach Irina. Otherwise, you can reach Irina by purchasing the Grave Key from the Shrine Maiden after giving the maiden the Mortician's Ashes
  • Walk into the tower to find Siegward of Catarina. Get on the elevator which goes down to the basement. Kill the Boreal Outrider Knight and rest at the Road of Sacrifices bonfire. To reach the top of the tower, hit the switch on the elevator while at the main entrance and then quickly get off to reveal a second lift. At the top of the tower, offer a token of friendship to the Giant of the Undead Settlement so that he does not attack you with giant arrows and even assists you when you are next a White Birch. Take the lift down, and while facing the entrance of the tower, when you hear Siegward's voice, roll off to land onto a wooden platform. Head outside and speak to Siegward again. Lure the the Fire Demon towards the tower which makes Siegward come and fight by your side. After killing the Fire Demon, speak to Siegward three times to receive the Siegbrau, Toast Gesture, and Sleep Gesture
  • Around the town, you can grab a Homeward Bone and a Large Club. You can also get the Northern Armor Set from a nearby hanging corpse
  • A Pale Tongue can be found on a nearby hanging corpse. Obtaining the tongue here or from invading successfully leads to Leonhard returning to Firelink Shrine. He then gives you the Lift Chamber Key that opens the door below the Tower on the Wall bonfire of High Wall of Lothric. Kill Darkwraith imprisoned there to get a Red Eye Orb. Return to Firelink Shrine and talk to Leonhard to receive the Applause Gesture
  • Walk into the house to get 2 Red Bug Pellets. Walk upstairs and grab 2 Alluring Skulls. Walk across a bridge and into a house. Pillage a chest to get 4 Human Pine Resin. Walk onto the roof and get Flynn's Ring
  • Drop down onto the ledge of a tower. Grab 2 Homeward Bones. Drop down from within the tower to get the Mirrah Set without the mask and the Chloranthy Ring
  • Get back to the burning tree from the Undead Settlement bonfire. Walk through a large archway of the house, open a door, and walk into the room. Cut down the bodies hanging over the staircase to get a Large Soul of a Deserted Corpse. Drop down from a hole in the room. In this hidden room, grab Warrior of Sunlight and consume Estus Soup
  • Open the door and walk to the end of the hallway. You should now be on the main street of the neighborhood. Head right and break through the wooden crates between the first gap on your right. Grab a Soul of an Unknown Traveler. Open the door and walk to the end of the hallway
  • Walk back the main street and proceed. A wooden door should automatically break. Walk into that house to get the Whip
  • Proceed back onto the main street. Walk until you see a wooden wheel to your right. Walk around the corner to get a Titanite Shard
  • Climb up the balcony with an Evangelist. Grab a Titanite Shard
  • Climb down from the balcony and walk onto a rooftop. Jump onto the wooden platform on your left to grab 2 Rusted Coins
  • Jump down to kill the Crystal Lizard which drops a Sharp Gem
  • Mad dark spirit Holy Knight Hodrick will invade. Kill him for his Vertebra Shackle
  • Enter the graveyard pierced by giant arrows and walk towards the house on the left. Grab the Mortician's Ashes, the Cleric Set, and a Blue Wooden Shield. If dodging the giant arrows is too troublesome, head to the tower to befriend the Giant
  • Grab all the items around the White Birch including the Undead Bone Shard on the platform to the right of the White Birch
  • Enter the house and walk upstairs. Grab the Great Scythe. Continue to walk towards the stone building and open the doors on your right
  • Return to Firelink Shrine. Give the Shrine Handmaid the Mortician's Ashes and then purchase the Grave Key which opens the locked door in the sewer area next to the Dilapidated Bridge bonfire
  • Go back the sewer area and open the locked door. Climb down and walk towards Velka the Goddess of Sin's statue to grab a Loincloth
  • Proceed through the tunnel to get the Red Hilted Halberd. Continue into another room to get a Soul of an Unknown Traveler
  • Walk outside to kill a Crystal Lizard which drops a Heavy Gem. Grab 2 Titanite Shards
  • Walk through the archway tunnel. Shoot down a hanging corpse to get a Blessed Red and White Shield+1
  • Walk into a new room and climb down to get a Saint's Talisman
  • Climb up and talk to Irina to receive the Prayer Gesture. Touch her and accept her services. She then moves to the Firelink Shrine. At the shrine, you can give her the Londor Braille Divine Tome, but doing so and buying any of its spells leads to her Dark ending. Otherwise, hang onto it until you find Karla later in the game
  • Kill the Curse-Rotted Greatwood to get the Transposing Kiln which Ludleth of Courtland can use to turn boss souls into weapons, rings, and spells
  • Talk to Hawkwood after killing the Greatwood to receive a Heavy Gem. If he does not give you anything, come back to him after killing another boss

Road of Sacrifices

  • Around now or before, you can now send Greirat to pillage Undead Settlement. He returns when you kill a boss
  • Find the Braille Divine Tome of Carim and Morne's Ring below the bridge right before the Halfway Fortress bonfire. Give the Tome to Irina at Firelink Shrine and buy all the spells it offers if you want her to become a Fire Keeper later in the game
  • Talk to Anri of Astora and Horace the Hushed at the Halfway Fortress bonfire. Talk to Horace to get the Blue Sentinels. Make sure to deplete Anri's dialogue before continuing the game
  • After meeting Anri of Astrora, return to Firelink Shrine to talk to Sirris of the Sunless Realms to begin her quest line
  • From the Crucifixion Woods bonfire, walk along the rocky wall towards the ruins until you reach a purple summon sign for mad phantom Holy Knight Hodrick. When summoned, he instantly becomes hostile, so kill him for souls
  • The Great Swamp Pyromancy Tome is in the deep part of the swamp near the entrance of Farron Keep
  • Dark spirit Yellowfinger Heysel invades around where the two giant crabs are. Kill her for Heysel Pick and Xanthous Crown
  • At the Halfway Fortress bonfire in Road of Sacrifices, head out and drop down from the ledges on the right side, keep going straight along the right wall and enter a doorway in the end. From your left, a Black Knight guards the Farron Coal. Walk along the ledge to the right of the knight to get the Sellsword Set
  • From the Crucifixion Woods bonfire, walk along the rocky wall towards the ruins until you reach a ledge. Fall down to grab the Estus Shard
  • Enter the ruins near the Crucifixion Woods bonfire, head up the stairs, and cross the bridge. Walk to the area to the left just after getting off the bridge which should lead you to the up to Orbeck of Vinheim. Talk to him and he agrees to teach you in sorceries only if you have at least 10 Intelligence and promise to bring him scrolls. He then moves to Firelink Shrine. If you do not bring him any scrolls before defeating 4 bosses, he leaves
  • Orbeck of Vinheim grants you different rewards based on certain criteria. The Young Dragon Ring is given to you after buying three of his spells and giving him at least one scroll. The Slumbering Dragon Ring is given to you after buying Aural Decoy, Farron Flashsword, and Pestilent Mercury. He will also give you the Silent Ally Gesture
  • In the room just before walking uphill to face the boss, near one of the stone pillars, Eygon of Carim has casted down a summon sign. You can summon him if you have recruited Irina to your Shrine and have not harmed her
  • Defeat the Crystal Sage

Cathedral of the Deep

  • Near the Cathedral of the Deep bonfire behind a hostile sword-wielding NPC is the Paladin's Ashes
  • Kill the axe-wielding NPC upstairs to get Spider Shield
  • Grab the Estus Shard right outside the Cleansing Chapel bonfire where enemies are praying
  • The Deep Braille Divine Tome can be found on a mimic chest. Remember, giving this to Irina and buying any of the spells it unlocks will lock you into the bad ending of her quest. I recommend saving this for Karla, unless you really want some of the spells
  • Grab the Undead Bone Shard near a white tree that the Giant throws spears around, on the roof area of the Cathedral after navigating the graveyard and crossing a bridge
  • Kill Longfinger Kirk, who will invade your world if you are embered on the ground floor of the cathedral between the area where the two giants are. He drops the Barbed Straight Sword and Spiked Shield, you will then be able to pick up the Armor of Thorns set in Rosaria's chamber
  • Note: If you want to encounter Patches in Cathedral of the Deep, do not go to the upper rafters (where you drop down to Rosaria) until you've completed his encounter, or he will not appear
  • Continue through the Cathedral of the Deep until you have made it to the fog door before the Deacons of the Deep boss fight. Head to the nearby lift to activate the final shortcut of the level (Opening the large blue double doors that lead out to the Cathedral's roof)
  • If you leave the Cathedral completely (Go to Firelink Shrine and come back) and then head back into the Cathedral through the large blue double doors you just opened, you may notice that a new bridge has been activated and Siegward is standing next to them. Talk to him and then cross the bridge to trigger a cutscene where it is revealed it was really Patches. Depending on if you killed the Giants or not, you'll either have to fight one or nothing happens
  • From here, head out to the 2nd shortcut back to the Cleansing Chapel bonfire and use the lift to get to a crossbow undead's tower. Kill him and move towards the edge of the tower, but don't drop down. Instead, look to see that you can move towards the left and find a ladder. Ascend the ladder and continue up the roof to eventually reach the rafters of the Cathedral. You'll now be able to drop down to the other side of the bridge that Patches activated. Patches will be waiting here and pretend to not be responsible for what just happened. Tell him "You know who I am" to receive the Prostration Gesture. You can then buy Siegward's Armor Set from him. NOTE: Purchasing this armor means Patches will not be a rescue option for Greirat later in Irithyll of the Boreal Valley
  • Before leaving this area, continue on up the nearby stairs and you'll eventually find yourself at the Rosaria's Chamber bonfire. Speak with Rosaria to obtain the Rosaria's Fingers covenant. Do not do anything further with this covenant if you intend to do Sirris' quest line. Don't give Rosaria any Pale Tongues and don't equip the Rosaria's Fingers covenant item. We'll continue this side quest closer to the end of the game
  • If Patches didn't spawn at the Cathedral, return to Firelink Shrine and purchase the Tower Key if you haven't already and head into the Tower behind Firelink Shrine now. As you ascend the lift to the top, you'll hear a door shut behind you. Grab the Fire Keeper's Soul at the top and then come back down to encounter Patches. He has locked the door and you'll need to drop down from the bridge onto coffins to escape
  • Head back to the Tower and get to the bridge that overlooks Firelink Shrine. Drop down onto the roof and move around the roof until you find some windows you can go through. An Estus Shard will be on the rafters
  • While on the roof, you can talk to Snuggly the Crow. Trading him a Homeward Bone will get you the Call Over Gesture
  • With the Fire Keeper's Soul, you can heal Dark Sigils that you have in order to completely get rid of your Hollowing Level. If you are following Yoel/Yuria's quest line, however, don't do this until the post game or you'll end up angering Yoel/Yuria
  • Refresh Firelink Shrine and head to the upper right to find Patches now located there. He'll ask for forgiveness, refuse to receive the Prostration Gesture and Rusted Gold Coin. You can then buy Siegward's Armor Set from him. Leaving Firelink Shrine and returning will sometimes allow Patches to be in his squatting position. Interact with him to receive the Patches Squat Gesture
  • If you can't buy Siegward's Armor Set yet, head to the Cleansing Chapel and check the well at the entrance, where Siegward should trapped. After speaking to him, return to whatever location Patches is at for you and buy the armor
  • Return to the well Siegward is in and drop the armor it to him to receive the Rejoice Gesture
  • Defeat the Deacons of the Deep

Farron Keep

  • After defeating the Deacons of the Deep, return to Firelink Shrine to find Anri and Horace. You can speak to them for some additional dialogue
  • From the first bonfire of Farron Keep, stay to the left side until you reach a small structure guarded by a darkwraith. The Sage's Coal is in a body inside this structure, which grants you Crystal, Blessed and Deep Infusions
  • Grab the Estus Shard as you come into the area from Road of Sacrifices, towards the left. The shard will be visible against a wall
  • Spawn at the Keep Ruins bonfire and go out towards the Wolf Gate and turn left. Follow the wall and you'll eventually find the Sage's Scroll
  • From the first bonfire, head out and go towards the right where you'll start to encounter basilisks. There will be a cave in this area that will contain a chest with the Golden Scroll inside. Make sure to give him at least one scroll before beating the Abyss Watchers or he'll leave forever
  • Grab the Undead Bone Shard on the bottom of the tower to the right of the Wolf Gate with the three flames that you have to light. It is surrounded by a bunch of slugs
  • Climb the giant ladder near the Keep Ruins bonfire to find the Old Wolf of Farron. Interact with him for the Watchdogs of Farron covenant to receive the Legion Etiquette Gesture
  • In this room, you'll see a dead body with something on it. Head out to the stairs and slash a wall once you get up them to reveal a illusory wall that leads to the body to get Dreamchaser's Ashes
  • Return to Firelink Shrine and after giving the Dreamchaser's Ashes to the Shrine Handmaid, refresh the world with a bonfire. Sirris will once again be there. Talk to her to receive the Darkmoon Loyalty Gesture
  • You can summon Yellowfinger Heysel in Farron Keep right after the Keep Ruins bonfire for the Abyss Watchers fight if you killed her in Road of Sacrifices, but you'll need to have given a Pale Tongue to Rosaria, which will block you out of Sirris' quest line, so I recommend saving this part for another playthrough. You'll gain the Proper Bow Gesture after her summon
  • Black Hand Gotthard can be summoned for the Abyss Watchers fight and will grant you the By My Sword Gesture upon summon
  • If you are doing the Yoel/Yuria side quest, you can also summon the Londor Pale Shade for the Abyss Watchers fight and gain the Duel Bow Gesture
  • Defeat the Abyss Watchers

Catacombs of Carthus

  • Return to Firelink Shrine and talk with Hawkwood to receive the Farron Ring. If Hawkwood isn't in the Shrine, head outside to the grave that normally has an undead dog by it and he may be there
  • Warp to a different zone and return to Firelink Shrine. If Hawkwood is no longer present, Hawkwood's Shield will be on the ground by a gravestone outside of Firelink Shrine, near where the dog spawns
  • About halfway across the first bridge of Catacombs of Carthus, you'll notice a platform you can drop down to on the left side. Do so and you'll find the Carthus Pyromancy Tome in that area
  • Before heading down the giant staircase that a skeleton ball will eventually begin to roll up and down, there will be a hallway to the left you can take. Head down this way and you'll eventually run into Anri, who claims she is looking for Horace. Exhaust her dialogue
  • Once arriving at the bottom of the giant staircase, you'll notice a skeleton with a hat on its head. Kill it and the next time the skeleton ball hits the bottom gate, it'll explode and reveal an Undead Bone Shard
  • After finding the Catacombs of Carthus bonfire, head down the stairs with the skeleton ball and then head up some other stairs across the way. Once you're at the top of this new area, head to the left and you'll find the Grave Warden's Ashes
  • Once you make it to the wooden bridge, you'll see Anri above and to the right of it. She'll once again ask if you have found Horace. You haven't yet, but we're about to. Head to the other side of the bridge and slash at it to cut it down and you can then use it as a ladder to descend into the Shouldering Lake
  • From the Catacombs Of Carthus bonfire, follow the skeleton ball and enter the empty jail to the right where you will be invaded by Knight Slayer Tsorig. Regardless of who wins the fight, you will receive the My Thanks! Gesture. Defeating him will grant you the Knight Slayer's Ring
  • Defeat High Lord Wolnir
  • After beating Wolnir, you'll obtain the Grave Warden Pyromancy Tome. Cornyx can't read this Pyromancy Tome, so you'll have to wait until you find Karla to turn it in

Smouldering Lake

  • When heading towards the Old Demon King fog door, a Carthus Sandworm will appear out of the ground. It may be a difficult fight with the ballista active, but you can use the ballista to your advantage to deal extra damage to the worm. Upon killing the Carthus Sandworm, you'll gain the Lightning Stake miracle and an Undead Bone Shard
  • Obtain the Speckled Stoneplate Ring which is located on a corpse in Smouldering Lake behind a brick wall along a large rock formation. You will need to trick the ballista into destroying the brick wall
  • Head into the area where the Demon Ruins bonfire is as this'll be where the bulk of the main items for this area are

Help

Is this a guide?

Nope, not really. This is a set of checklists and information that you can use while playing Dark Souls to make sure you don't miss an item, action, conversation or boss. If you need playthroughs then you can click on the links scattered throughout the page to find them in the wiki.

Do I need to follow the playthrough in this exact order?

Dark Souls 3 is not a linear game and has multiple ways of progressing. This is generally the way I like to play through the game, so feel free to follow it if you like or mix it up. You can always jump back and forth and check things off in a different order.

Can I use this for multiple characters?

Yup, use the profile selector and buttons at the top right of the page to setup multiple characters.

How do I help with feedback, corrections, suggestions or additions?

You can visit the GitHub repository and report Issues or create a fork and submit a Pull Request. You can also post in the Reddit thread, but you will receive a slower response.

Thanks to the many contributors for their suggestions, fixes and improvements

How does the checklist status get saved?

It gets saved to the local storage in your browser and not to any database or API. So if you are clearing your browser cache make sure not to delete the site data or your checklists will get reset.
